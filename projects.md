# Current and Recent Research Projects

<div class="project-section">

  <div class="project-card featured">
    <div class="project-logo">
      <img src="images/projects/UofC.png" alt="University of Calgary logo">
    </div>
    <div class="project-content">
      <div class="project-header">
        <h3>Representation Learning for Unstructured Genetic Data</h3>
        <span>University of Calgary</span>
      </div>

      <p class="project-subtitle">
        PhD Research Project
      </p>

      <p>
        This research develops novel biostatistical and representation-learning methods for identifying
        genetic factors underlying complex diseases, including cancer and cardiovascular disorders.
        The project bridges statistical genetics, deep learning, and explainable AI to improve the
        interpretation of disease biology, polygenic architecture, and heritability.
      </p>

      <div class="project-tags">
        <span>Statistical Genetics</span>
        <span>Deep Learning</span>
        <span>Explainable AI</span>
        <span>Polygenic Analysis</span>
        <span>Heritability</span>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="project-card">
    <div class="project-logo">
      <img src="images/projects/UofC.png" alt="University of Calgary logo">
    </div>
    <div class="project-content">
      <div class="project-header">
        <h3>Analysis of Multi-Scale Data</h3>
        <span>University of Calgary</span>
      </div>

      <p class="project-subtitle">
        Research Project
      </p>

      <p>
        This project focused on the analysis and interpretation of multi-scale data using a real-world
        survival-data application. The goal was to understand how information measured at different
        biological or clinical scales can be integrated into a coherent statistical analysis framework.
      </p>

      <div class="project-tags">
        <span>Multi-scale Data</span>
        <span>Survival Analysis</span>
        <span>Biostatistics</span>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="project-card">
    <div class="project-logo">
      <img src="images/projects/UofC.png" alt="University of Calgary logo">
    </div>
    <div class="project-content">
      <div class="project-header">
        <h3>Survival Analysis of Heart Failure Patients</h3>
        <span>University of Calgary</span>
      </div>

      <p class="project-subtitle">
        Research Project
      </p>

      <p>
        This project applied survival-analysis methods to heart-failure patient data in order to study
        time-to-event outcomes and identify clinical factors associated with patient prognosis.
      </p>

      <div class="project-tags">
        <span>Survival Analysis</span>
        <span>Clinical Data</span>
        <span>Heart Failure</span>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="project-card">
    <div class="project-logo">
      <img src="images/projects/UofC.png" alt="University of Calgary logo">
    </div>
    <div class="project-content">
      <div class="project-header">
        <h3>Predicting Phenotypes from Genotypes</h3>
        <span>University of Calgary</span>
      </div>

      <p class="project-subtitle">
        Research Project
      </p>

      <p>
        This project compared multiple statistical and machine-learning models for predicting phenotypic
        traits from genotype data. Methods included regularized regression approaches such as ridge
        regression and lasso, with the broader goal of evaluating how genetic variation can be used for
        predictive modeling of observable traits.
      </p>

      <div class="project-tags">
        <span>Genotype Data</span>
        <span>Phenotype Prediction</span>
        <span>Ridge Regression</span>
        <span>Lasso</span>
        <span>Machine Learning</span>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>

</div>
